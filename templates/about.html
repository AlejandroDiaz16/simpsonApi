What is this?

The simpsons Api is a service that icludes all the data in the simpsons universe.

Most the people have seen the simpsons and really enjoy each chapter, doesn't matter if is the fourth or fifth time that you whatch it. Also we know there are a lot of companies, insitutions and programmers that test apps even web apps or local programms with data that they have to fill or request. For those reasons, we present to you all the Characters, Episodes, Locations and Cities of the simpsons Universe.

We have formatted this data in JSON and exposed it to you with a RESTful app.

Check out the documentation to get started consuming the simpsons data

What can I use this for?

Even if you are a company or just someone that recenty started to learn programming you all can use this api to test your apps or make and app that is related with or needs this data for a long time you won't spend time making an Api or searching all the information about characters or other series, you are going to have all the information just with one consult of distance.

Who are you?

We are Sergio bustos, a guy who likes to develop anykind of app and Jair Ayala, the person in charge of maintain the "Simpsons Api" database updated. We both love the simpsons and whe know there are a lot of people that love them too.

Technical stuff?

We use Flask and sqlite to serve the API. All the data is formatted in JSON and the entire project is hosted on pythonanywhere. However in order to optimize the Api the images are located into a especial server for images.

How can i contribute to the project?

you can help us to keep the project alive with any donation or you can also contribute on Github.
